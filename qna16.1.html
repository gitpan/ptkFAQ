16.1 Is there a Tcl/Tk to perl/Tk translator?

Nick Ing-Simmons has written a (rather lengthy) tcl2perl script. It is being distributed with the perl/Tk code as of Tk-b9.01. Please handle carefully! (translation: do not expect it to translate arbitrary tcl code accurately nor even into the most efficient perl/Tk equivalent. Do go over the converted script with care - and do not forget -w and use strict;.) Thanks Nick :-)
